Start your sponsorship journey today with these simple and effective steps designed to maximize your impact!

1

Get Started with Y-WE

Begin your sponsorship journey by booking a free consultation through our website. After the meeting, you'll receive an email with login details to access your personalized dashboard. This dashboard is your all-in-one hub for managing sponsorship opportunities and growing your business effectively.

2

Explore Recommended Offers and Matches

Log in to discover sponsorship opportunities tailored to your marketing goals. Our AI will recommend personalized sponsorships based on your consultation and data insights. 3

Deal!

Select your ideal sponsorship offer and complete the deal seamlessly. Make a secure payment through our platform to access direct communication with the sponsored partner. Finalize the details and digitally sign the sponsorship agreement using our secure tools. Funds are transferred to the sponsored partner only after all conditions are met, ensuring trust and transparency.

4

Post-Event Review and Feedback

After the sponsored event concludes, we’ll help you evaluate the sponsorship’s success by reviewing performance metrics such as brand visibility and audience reach. If agreed-upon outcomes aren’t met, we’ll assist with refunds or alternative solutions. Finally, share your feedback to refine future sponsorship recommendations and enhance your results.
